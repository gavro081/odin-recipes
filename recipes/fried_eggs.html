Fried Eggs

Click for recipe!

Ingredients:

  • 1 tbsp. butter, or fat of your choice
  • 1 large egg
  • Kosher salt
  • Freshly ground black pepper
  1. In a small nonstick over medium heat, melt butter (or heat oil).
  2. Crack egg into pan. Cook 3 minutes, or until white is set. Flip and cook 30 seconds until white is just set. Remove from pan and season with salt and pepper.
